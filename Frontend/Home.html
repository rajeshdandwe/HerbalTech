what the websites is about?

T This website utilizes advanced image processing and machine learning to accurately identify medicinal plants and raw materials for Ayurvedic pharmaceutics. Its primary goal is to address industry challenges like misidentification and adulteration. Users upload images for precise analysis, benefiting herbalists, pharmacists, manufacturers, researchers, and consumers. Herbalists and pharmacists can verify authenticity, while manufacturers enhance quality control. Researchers access a vast database for botanical studies, and consumers gain insight into medicinal properties. With a user-friendly interface, it ensures transparency and integrity in herbal medicine. This platform promotes traditional knowledge preservation and innovation in the herbal industry.
